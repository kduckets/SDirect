About ReString Club

ReString Club is the premier, subscription guitar string delivery service for the player who demands professional grade strings at a price that makes sense. Our strings are made with machine precision in the same factories as the strings on your guitar right now.

Pay less, play more and choose how often you want strings. Twice a month? Every other month? No problem. Going on the road? Put your membership on hold at anytime with one click! Our mission is music and that starts with great tone. The rest is up to you... Enjoy!
